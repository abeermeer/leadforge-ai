**Version 1.0 — 23 July 2026**

These terms govern use of the LeadForge AI platform ("the Platform") operated by

agree to them.

> **Not legal advice.** This is an operator-drafted acceptable-use policy that
> defines the boundary between your responsibility and ours. Have a qualified

## 1. Who is responsible for what

**You are the sender.** The Platform is a tool that composes and transmits
messages on your instruction, from your sending domain, on your behalf. You are
the data controller and the legally responsible sender for every message.

**We provide the mechanism**, including unsubscribe handling, suppression
enforcement, and sending-rate controls. We do not review your recipients, your
copy, or your right to contact anyone.

## 2. Lawful use

You warrant that you will:

- Contact only businesses and individuals you have a lawful basis to contact
  (legitimate interest, consent, or an existing business relationship — whichever
  your jurisdiction requires).
- Comply with all applicable law in your jurisdiction and the recipient's,
  including **CAN-SPAM** (US), **GDPR / ePrivacy** (EU/UK), **CASL** (Canada),
  and any local equivalents.
- Send only commercial outreach that accurately identifies you, your business,
  and the reason for contact.
- Include a valid physical postal address (the Platform requires one before it
  will send).

## 3. Prohibited

You will **not**:

- Import, upload, or send to **purchased, rented, scraped-for-resale, or
  otherwise third-party-sourced contact lists**.
- Send to any address that has unsubscribed, bounced, or reported a previous
  message as spam — from this Platform or anywhere else.
- Use false, misleading, or disguised sender names, reply-to addresses, subject
  lines, or message headers.
- Send content that is unlawful, deceptive, defamatory, harassing, adult, or
  promotes illegal goods or services.
- Attempt to bypass the Platform's suppression list, rate limits, sending
  window, or unsubscribe mechanism.
- Use the Platform to send on behalf of an undisclosed third party.

## 4. Unsubscribes and suppression

- Every message carries a one-click unsubscribe link, a `List-Unsubscribe`
  header, and your postal address.
- Unsubscribes, bounces, and spam complaints are recorded in a **permanent
  suppression list** and enforced before every send, including follow-ups.
- **Suppression is permanent and cannot be undone by re-importing a contact.**
  Deleting a contact's personal data does not remove them from suppression — we
  retain a one-way hash of the address specifically so that erasure can never
  make someone contactable again.
- A reply, bounce, or unsubscribe automatically cancels any remaining scheduled
  follow-ups.

## 5. Sending reputation

- You are responsible for your sending domain's authentication (**SPF, DKIM,
  DMARC**) and for its reputation.
- We may throttle, pause, or suspend sending on your account if your bounce
  rate, spam-complaint rate, or recipient behaviour threatens the deliverability
  of the Platform or other clients.
- Warmup limits and daily/hourly caps are safety mechanisms, not obstacles.
  Circumventing them is a breach of these terms.

## 6. Data protection

- **Retention.** Lead and email-log data is automatically purged after the
  configured retention window (default **24 months**). Suppression records are
  retained indefinitely as a legal do-not-contact record.
- **Erasure.** A data-subject deletion request is honoured by an erasure that
  removes the contact's personal data across leads, email logs, sequences, and
  cached audit data, while preserving their hashed suppression entry.
- **Your account.** Deleting your account permanently removes your data,
  including all leads, campaigns, logs, and stored credentials.
- **Credentials.** Third-party API keys you supply are encrypted at rest and are
  never returned to you in full.

## 7. Third-party data sources

The Platform gathers publicly available business information (websites, search
results, business directories, advertising transparency libraries). You are
responsible for ensuring your use of that information is lawful in your
jurisdiction and for honouring any objection or erasure request you receive
about it.

## 8. Suspension

We may suspend or terminate access immediately, without refund, for breach of
sections 2, 3, or 5 — in particular for sending to purchased lists or for
attempting to bypass suppression.

## 9. Liability

The Platform is provided "as is". You indemnify us against claims, penalties,
and losses arising from your use of it, including regulatory action relating to
messages you sent.
